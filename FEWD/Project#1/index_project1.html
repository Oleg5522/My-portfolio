HOMEMADE CHOCOLATE RECIPE

Preamble

After an experimental 20-day fast, I became very interested in recipes and preparing my favorite foods. One of them is chocolate. I bought the necessary ingredients at the market, mixed and prepared them, then poured the hot mixture into molds and I got chocolate, which was no different from what you can buy in a supermarket, the cost and price of which is much more expensive due to mass production. When I treated my friends, they sometimes said to me: “I have never tasted more delicious chocolate in my life!”


My chocolate bar on the scale

Ingredients

There are only three main ingredients:

  1. Cocoa butter (Click here if you don't know what it looks like).
  2. Cocoa mass (Click here if you don't know what it looks like).
  3. Natural honey (Click here if you don't know what it looks like).

I also added various spices:

  • Vanilla
  • Cardamom
  • Cinnamon

Recipe

The recipe is quite simple: Initially, the recipe for homemade chocolate was as follows: cocoa mass 300 g, cocoa butter 100 g, honey 150-180 g, spices. Later, I reduced the main proportions of cocoa mass and cocoa butter to 2:1 due to the fact that I do not like dark chocolate. Everything is done in a water bath, stirring, then poured into molds. Then put it in the refrigerator and after 8 hours your chocolate is ready!
