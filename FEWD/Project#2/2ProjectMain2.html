Adventures in Africa

It was a new era in my life. Africa is a different world. It has different laws, different food, different mentality, different air... I have received so much stress in several years of working life in Africa, as I have never received before in all my jobs. I have had about ten life-threatening situations in my life, and the most dangerous of them happened in Africa, where I was kidnapped by four local gungsters. Perhaps then God was merciful to me and made it so that I now have the opportunity to write these lines.. After a long time of fighting with various local government services, law enforcement agencies, bandits and shamans, we were forced to stop working our plant. After that, I performed a feat, "pulling out" from the state budget a VAT refund of almost a billion francs and left Africa, which had become as my second home, I think, forever...
