Introduction

Welcome to my personal page!
My name is Oleg. I am an economist by education and the main part of my working life was connected with accounting, taxation, auditing and finance.
I studied quite a lot, I was the best among the students. I got a red diploma and naively thought that it gave me the green light in my professional life. I was disappointed when my first interviews let me know that in my country talents are not needed. I was forced to work as a courier for a long time and only a year later, after I accidentally met my teacher, I was hired as a chief accountant in a publishing center with a salary of 70 dollars a month. When my future director at that time offered me such money, my eyes involuntarily turned into a five-kopeck coin , because, working as a courier, I earned only 15 dollars a month.
This is how my professional life path began.
